About Us!

  We're just 4 guys who really love Smash Mouth, and learned to play a few instruments! When Shrek first came out in 2001, we just couldn't get their song 'All Star' out of our heads. John taught himself how to shred on guitar and Jimmy knew how to hold a beat on a drumset. Then they brought me (John) in to sing and we hired Gregory off of Fiver to come play bass and he ended up sticking around. For awhile, we just played at my niece's birthday party every year, but now we take a few gigs in the area. If you're interested in having some Smash Mouth vibes at your next event, head over to the contact page!
